Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our treatments

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

We usually begin with a universal first-time facial that suits most skin types. After your initial session, our skincare specialist will assess your skin's response, discuss your goals, and recommend a tailored plan to help you achieve your best results.

Why can I only book a basic facial to start?

We recommend starting with a foundational facial to introduce your skin to our treatments. After evaluating how your skin responds, our specialists will guide you toward options that target your unique concerns more effectively.

What can I expect during my first visit?

You'll be greeted by a licensed skincare professional who will go over your medical history, prepare your skin, and perform your initial treatment. The process typically takes about 15 minutes. Afterward, you'll receive personalized recommendations for follow-up treatments and skincare products.

What types of facials do you offer?

We offer a variety of treatments including laser facials, energy-based resurfacing, and combination therapies. Each option is designed to target specific concerns while remaining suitable for all skin types and tones.

How often should I get a facial?

The frequency depends on your skin's needs and your overall goals. Our team will create a personalized treatment schedule based on your skin type and concerns to help you see consistent, long-lasting results.

Who performs the facials?

All of our treatments are carried out by licensed professionals who have undergone specialized training in skincare procedures. They work under the supervision of experienced dermatological professionals and follow strict protocols to ensure safety and effectiveness.
